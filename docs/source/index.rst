Next Generation Repository Infrastructure at the University of Tennessee
========================================================================

This document describes the infrastructure the University of Tennessee envisions for its next generation repositories.
It describes our vision for the services we will use and support in the future and how they fit together.

While this document is subject to change, it is intended to help orient us in regards to where we are going and what our
infrastructure may look like in the near future.
